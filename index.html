My process for creating human-centric and functional solutions includes...

Research

Information is collected, problems are researched, and user needs & frustrations are analyzed with the help of market research, user surveys & competetive analysis.

Content Creation

Analysed data gets translated into tasks, ideas and features as the user stories, user flows, site maps, and content strategy are produced and put together.

Wireframes

During this phase the information created gets assembled on a page, first through sketches and then a digital form.

Branding

Now, the product gets its visual identity. Logos are created, moodboards & style guides are refereced and preference tests are run.

Prototyping & Usability Testing

The screens are connected together and usability testing starts to ensure that the product is intuitive, comprehensive and effective for the user.

Front End

The visual design get translated responsive design through HTML, CSS and JavaScript and gets pushed to GitHub.
